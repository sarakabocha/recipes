Challah recipe

Delicious and foolproof challah bread!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Honey
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Combine the wet ingredients.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until well combined. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Fold the dough over itself, rotating the bowl by a quarter, 8 folds in total. Cover with plastic wrap and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  5. Repeat the previous step 4 more times.
  6. Store the dough in the fridge overnight, for 16-24 hours.
  7. Roll out the dough onto a well-floured surface. Fold it over a few times to round out the shape.
  8. Make 3 strands of dough, and braid them together.
  9. Let the loaf sit for an hour or more at room temperature, until it's proofed enough. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  10. Bake the loaf for about 30 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  11. Remove from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes.
  12. Enjoy!
